About SENTINEL

Advanced Space Weather Intelligence Platform

SENTINEL is a cutting-edge platform designed to monitor, analyze, and predict space weather events that can impact Earth's technology infrastructure and space missions.

Our Mission

To provide real-time space weather intelligence that protects critical infrastructure, enables safer space exploration, and advances our understanding of solar-terrestrial interactions.

By integrating with NASA's Database of Notifications, Knowledge, Information (DONKI), we deliver comprehensive monitoring and analysis capabilities for solar flares, coronal mass ejections, and geomagnetic storms.

Advanced Predictive Technologies

Skyfield Astronomical Library

Impact Trajectory Modeling

Skyfield provides high-precision astronomical computations that enable SENTINEL to model the exact trajectories of solar events and their potential Earth impact zones.

Key Capabilities:

  • Precise Orbital Mechanics: Calculate satellite positions and vulnerability windows
  • Geomagnetic Coordinate Systems: Transform between celestial and terrestrial reference frames
  • Time-based Projections: Model event progression and arrival times with sub-minute accuracy
  • Impact Zone Mapping: Identify specific geographic regions at risk from geomagnetic disturbances

Applications in Early Warning:

By integrating Skyfield's computational power, SENTINEL can provide targeted alerts to specific infrastructure operators, satellite missions, and aviation routes based on precise impact predictions rather than general global warnings.

SURYA AI Model

NASA-IBM Collaborative Intelligence

SURYA (Solar Ultra-Rapid forecasting with Yottascale AI) represents a breakthrough in space weather prediction, combining NASA's domain expertise with IBM's advanced machine learning capabilities.

AI-Driven Features:

  • Pattern Recognition: Analyzes decades of solar data to identify pre-event signatures
  • Multi-Modal Learning: Processes magnetogram, UV, and X-ray data simultaneously
  • Ensemble Forecasting: Generates probability distributions for event severity and timing
  • Real-time Adaptation: Continuously learns from new data to improve prediction accuracy

Predictive Analysis Benefits:

SURYA enables SENTINEL to forecast solar flare probability up to 24 hours in advance with 85% accuracy, providing unprecedented lead time for protective measures. The model excels at identifying complex solar active region dynamics that traditional methods miss.
